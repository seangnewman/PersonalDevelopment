Music of My Mind

Stevie Wonder's official Fan Page

I was blessed with the opportunity to meet Stevie Wonder back in the day when I lived (briefly) in Los Angeles. It was a brief encounter, probably one of thousands he has had throughout his 50+ years as one of the leading musicians of his era. While passionate about his craft, I was struck by how gracious he was with his time and how willing he was to share with us his ideas on issues such as social justice and poverty.

Although generally regarded as an R&B artist, Stevie Wonder has always incorportated a number of different genre's into his music. Touring with the Rolling Stones and introducing himself to their audience was a primary driver to the success of "Superstition". In additon to the harmonica and keyboards, Stevie Wonder has also showcased his drum abilities frequently.

Throughout my life I have assumed a number of roles; son, brother, father, friend, husband. Throughout this time the music of Stevie Wonder has been a constant. In many ways his music has become a soundtrack of my life.
